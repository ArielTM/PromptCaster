Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Summary: PromptCaster does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data stays on your device.

What Data We Collect

PromptCaster does not collect any personal information or usage data. The extension operates entirely locally on your device.

Host Permissions

PromptCaster requests access to AI service websites (such as ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.) to:

  • Inject prompts you write into the AI chat interfaces
  • Read responses from these services for side-by-side comparison

This access is used solely to enable the core functionality of comparing AI responses. We do not:

  • Track your browsing history
  • Store your conversations
  • Send any data to external servers

Local Storage

The extension may use your browser's local storage to save:

  • Your preferences and settings
  • Window positioning data

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the extension or clearing browser data.

Third-Party Services

When you use PromptCaster to interact with AI services, your prompts are sent directly to those services (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic, Google). These interactions are governed by the respective privacy policies of those services.

PromptCaster itself does not act as an intermediary and has no access to your API keys or account credentials for these services.

Data Security

Since we don't collect or transmit any data, there is no risk of data breaches from our side. Your information remains secure on your local device.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
